About MBE

Based in KwaMhlanga, Mpumalanga, MBE Glass and Aluminium has been a trusted name since 2019.
We specialize in crafting high-quality aluminium windows, doors, and garages,
tailored to enhance the beauty and functionality of your home or business.
Our commitment to innovation and craftsmanship drives us to deliver custom solutions that meet your unique needs.

What We Do

We design, assemble, and install premium aluminium products, offering a full-service experience from concept to completion.
Our expertise includes:

  • Aluminium Windows: Sleek, energy-efficient designs like sliding and casement windows to brighten your space.
  • Aluminium Doors: Stylish and secure options, including sliding, pivot, and folding doors.
  • Garages: Durable, modern aluminium garages for residential and commercial properties.

Our skilled team ensures every product is built with precision and installed with care, guaranteeing lasting performance.

Why Choose MBE?

  • Mpumalanga Expertise: We know South Africa’s climate and deliver products suited to local conditions.
  • Custom Solutions: Every project is unique, designed to match your vision.
  • Complete Service: From design consultation to installation, we handle it all.
  • Trusted Since 2019: Our reputation is built on quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Journey

Established in 2019, MBE Glass and Aluminium began as a family-driven venture in Mpumalanga with a mission to provide innovative aluminium solutions.
From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a leading provider of custom windows, doors, and garages,
earning the trust of clients across the region through our dedication to excellence.
